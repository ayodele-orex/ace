Brief History

Ace Supermarket started as a small seed in 2012/13 in order to plug the gap the CEO observed in Akobo area of Ibadan and to generate employment. Today, Ace Supermarket has become a household name spanning across major cities in Southwest Nigeria namely Ibadan, Oshogbo, Abeokuta, Oyo, Ogbomosho, Ijebu-Ode, Ile-Ife, and Ilorin.

Ace as the elite supermarket in all these cities has continued to be of tremendous service to the need of the people who in the past have had to travel to lagos or Ibadan in order to shop for their essential needs. With over 400 workforce, Ace Supermarket is one of the leading employers of labour in Southwest Nigeria. Ace services span accross Groceries, Bakery, Boutique, Eatery, Lounge and Cinema.
